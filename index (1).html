What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The canteen menu provides a selection of healthy, hot and cold options for lunch and recess for students to choose from. We try to keep all our menu items affordable and we regularly check the nutrition values of the products we use, with an emphasis on low sodium and low fat items. All sandwiches are made using wholemeal bread. White bread is available on request. No margarine will be used unless requested on your order. Students are also limited on the number of items they can buy for recess and lunch snack times.
